# 7 Simple & Healthy Weight Loss Meal Prep Ideas

Looking for easy meal prep ideas that support your weight loss goals without sacrificing flavor? These healthy recipes are simple to prepare, perfect for weekly planning, and packed with nutritious ingredients.

## 1. Grilled Chicken & Veggie Power Bowl

**Prep Time:** 25 mins | **Serves:** 3-4

### Ingredients:

- 2 grilled chicken breasts
- 1 cup steamed broccoli
- 1 cup sliced carrots
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes

### Prep Steps:

1. Season & grill chicken breasts until fully cooked (about 6–7 minutes per side)
2. Steam broccoli until tender but still bright green (about 5 minutes)
3. Slice carrots into coins and cherry tomatoes in halves
4. Let all ingredients cool, then assemble into containers with balanced portions

**Why it works:** High-protein, low-calorie, and rich in fiber for longer satiety.

## 2. Chickpea Curry with Rice & Quinoa

**Prep Time:** 30 mins | **Vegan-Friendly**

### Ingredients:

- 1 can chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
- 1/2 cup curry sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- 1 cup cooked white rice
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

### Prep Steps:

1. In a saucepan, simmer chickpeas in curry sauce for 10–12 minutes
2. Cook rice and quinoa separately as per package instructions
3. Allow all ingredients to cool before dividing into containers
4. Garnish with fresh cilantro before storing

**Why it works:** Balanced plant-based proteins and complex carbs support energy and digestion.

## 3. Fresh Veggie & Avocado Bowl

**Prep Time:** 15 mins | **Light & Fresh**

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1/2 cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 avocado, diced
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
- Fresh parsley

### Prep Steps:

1. Cook quinoa and let it cool completely
2. Slice cucumber, dice avocado, and halve cherry tomatoes
3. Add everything into your container, and top with chopped parsley
4. Optional: squeeze a bit of lemon or drizzle olive oil when serving

**Why it works:** This fiber-rich, healthy fat combo keeps you full while staying light.

## 4. Veggie Wraps with Roasted Zucchini

**Prep Time:** 20 mins | **High-fiber & filling**

### Ingredients:

- 1 whole wheat tortilla
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
- Olive oil, salt, and pepper

### Prep Steps:

1. Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper
2. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 15 minutes or until tender
3. Wrap veggies in the tortilla or store them separately in mini wraps
4. Optional: spread hummus or light cheese in the tortilla for extra flavor

**Why it works:** Whole grains plus roasted veggies = satisfying, nutrient-dense wrap.

## 5. Protein-Packed Chicken Stir-Fry

**Prep Time:** 20 mins | **Low-carb friendly**

### Ingredients:

- 1 chicken breast, sliced
- 1/2 bell pepper
- 1/2 onion
- Handful of snow peas
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos or soy sauce

### Prep Steps:

1. Slice and sauté chicken in a pan with a splash of oil until cooked
2. Add chopped veggies and stir-fry for 5–6 minutes
3. Pour in coconut aminos and cook for another minute
4. Let cool and pack into containers

**Why it works:** Protein-rich with fiber-packed veggies and flavor without sugar.

## 6. Lentil & Spinach Salad with Lemon Dressing

**Prep Time:** 15 mins | **Vegan & Gluten-Free**

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup cooked lentils
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1/4 cucumber, chopped
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
- Dressing: lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper

### Prep Steps:

1. Cook lentils (or use canned), and let them cool
2. Combine spinach, cucumber, and tomatoes in your container
3. Add lentils and store dressing separately or drizzle just before serving

**Why it works:** Lentils offer iron, protein, and fiber — ideal for staying full longer.

## 7. Greek Yogurt Parfait with Berries

**Prep Time:** 5 mins | **Easy Breakfast**

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup mixed berries
- 1 tsp chia seeds
- Optional: granola or honey

### Prep Steps:

1. In a jar or container, layer Greek yogurt and berries
2. Sprinkle chia seeds and optional granola
3. Store in the fridge — ready to grab for a healthy breakfast or snack!

**Why it works:** Protein and probiotics support gut health and weight loss.

## Quick Tips for Meal Prep Success

- Batch cook proteins and grains ahead of time
- Use airtight containers to maintain freshness
- Freeze extra portions to avoid spoilage and save time
- Track calories/macros if you're following a specific plan
- Rotate recipes weekly to keep meals exciting and prevent boredom
