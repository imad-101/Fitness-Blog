# 🥜 15 Delicious High-Calorie Smoothies for Healthy Weight Gain to Help You Bulk Up Fast

Whether you're looking to gain healthy weight, fuel your workouts, or simply enjoy something rich and nourishing, these high-calorie smoothies are your new best friends. Perfect for those following a weight gain meal plan for women, anyone transitioning out of calorie deficit recipes, or anyone in need of tasty weight gain meals that go down easy!

Each smoothie below includes ingredients, step-by-step instructions, calories per 100g of each ingredient, and the total estimated calories per shake.

## 1. 🍓 Strawberry Banana Power Shake

**Calories per serving:** ~530 kcal

### Ingredients:

- Banana (118g) – 105 kcal
- Strawberries (150g) – 48 kcal
- Whole milk (250ml) – 150 kcal
- Greek yogurt (100g) – 97 kcal
- Peanut butter (1 tbsp / 16g) – 94 kcal
- Honey (1 tsp / 7g) – 25 kcal

### Steps:

1. Add all ingredients to blender
2. Blend until smooth
3. Serve with fresh strawberry garnish
